About the Artist

David Fafard is a Parisian native born on January 24, 1964. With over three decades of professional experience, he relocated to the United States to expand his artistic horizons and establish a distinguished international presence.

Renowned for mastery in gemstone, marble, bronze, gold, and mixed media, David Fafard’s work has been commissioned and collected by private collectors, art galleries, museums, and professional institutions including law firms.

His sculptures explore the human form, abstract expression, animal figures, and fine art painting—each piece crafted as a timeless, one-of-a-kind work.
